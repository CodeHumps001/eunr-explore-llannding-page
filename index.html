Frequently Asked Questions

Online Application: Visit the UENR Admissions Portal and follow the step-by-step instructions to complete your application. You will need to upload your academic certificates, transcripts, and other required documents.

Requirements: Ensure you meet the minimum entry requirements for your chosen program. These may include WASSCE/SSSCE results, relevant certificates, and in some cases, an entrance examination.

Deadlines: Application periods are announced on the university website. Apply early to avoid missing out.

Support: For help, contact the admissions office at admissions@uenr.edu.gh or call +233 35 202 6477.

Undergraduate Programs: UENR offers degrees in Renewable Energy Engineering, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Natural Resources Management, Environmental Science, and more.

Postgraduate Programs: Master's and PhD programs are available in select fields such as Sustainable Energy, Environmental Management, and Applied Sciences.

Short Courses: The university also runs certificate and diploma courses for professional development.

Full List: Visit the Academic Programmes Page for a comprehensive list.

On-Campus Hostels: UENR provides secure and comfortable hostels for both male and female students. Facilities include study areas, internet access, and 24/7 security.

Off-Campus Options: There are approved private hostels near campus. The university assists students in finding suitable accommodation.

Application: Hostel spaces are limited and allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Apply for accommodation as soon as you receive admission.

Support: For details, contact the hostel manager at hostels@uenr.edu.gh.

Academic Facilities: Modern lecture halls, science and computer laboratories, and a well-equipped library.

Sports & Recreation: Sports arena, football fields, basketball courts, and fitness centers.

Student Life: Botanical gardens, cafeterias, student clubs, and cultural event spaces.

Technology: Free Wi-Fi zones, e-learning platforms, and IT support.

Health & Safety: Campus clinic, security services, and counseling centers.
